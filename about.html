About ZAPD

The Zimbabwe Assosiation of Pedriatic Dentistry is dedicated to the oral health of children from infancy through the teenage years. We are here to ensure every child gets the best start possible.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To advance the oral health of children and adolescents in Zimbabwe by promoting excellence in pediatric dentistry, supporting members through education, and advocating for accessible, compassionate care.

Vision

A Zimbabwe where every child—regardless of background or ability—receives timely preventive and restorative dental care in a safe, child-friendly environment.

We envision a national network of skilled pediatric dentists collaborating with communities, schools, and health systems to eliminate preventable dental suffering.

What We Stand For

Prevention first

Early visits, fluoride, sealants, and parent education reduce disease before it starts.

Evidence-based care

Clinical decisions grounded in current pediatric dental science.

Inclusion

Dedicated support for children with special health care needs.
